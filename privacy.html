Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Limiss Bot ("the Bot") collects, stores, and uses information while providing its services on Discord.

Information We Collect

The Bot may store:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server IDs
  • Discord Channel IDs
  • Discord Role IDs
  • Economy System Data
  • Leveling System Data
  • Moderation Data
  • Configuration Settings

How We Use Data

The collected information is used solely to provide the Bot's features, including moderation, economy systems, leveling systems, logging systems, and server configuration.

Data Sharing

Limiss Bot does not sell, rent, or share collected information with third parties.

Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored information, however no method of storage is completely secure.

Data Removal

Server administrators may request removal of stored server data by contacting the Bot owner.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the Bot constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the Bot owner through the support server.
